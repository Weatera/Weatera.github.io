AI painter

 

These two drawings are created from AI, from wombo art and gaugan art.

How to use wombo art: you type the things you want in the drawing, then you select the style you want, last you press create button, you will get a beautiful drawing.

How to use gaugan: First you need to tick the part that ask you the permission to use this. Then you choose a brush and choose a colour to drw at the left side area. Then you press the right arrow button to create a drawing.
